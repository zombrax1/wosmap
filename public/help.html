Help & Info

This page explains how to navigate the map and manage city spots.

How to Use

Browse the map to view existing city spots. Use zoom and drag to explore.

How to Insert a City

  1. Open the Map page and select Insert City.
  2. Fill in the city details including name, level, status, coordinates and optional notes.
  3. Click Save to add the city to the map.
